Role of a Student Ambassador

As a Student Ambassador (SA) of the Sustainable Education Foundation (SEF), you will act as a liaison for SEF while also acting as an Evangelist for the SEF brand among your peers. SAâ€™s are trained and mentored throughout the program to enable you to be career-ready. This position is for student leaders who are passionate about developing their employability skills and sharing knowledge.

Responsibilities and specific duties

Conduct at least three sessions to spread the word of SEF in your respective universities

Build a strong,sustainable SEF community and collaboration within other clubs/societies in your respective universities

Share the work that SEF is doing among your peers

Blog/Vlog about your experience with SEF(minimum of two posts every month)

Maintain the SEF brand name internally and externally from the University

Volunteer in a SEF core team/work stream

Skills Required

A high degree of multitasking and time management capability

Good communication skills in English and Sinhala or Tamil

Exemplary leadership qualities
